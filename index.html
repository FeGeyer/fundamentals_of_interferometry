Fundamentals of Radio Interferometry

An Introduction

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

This is the website for the NASSP Masters Fundamentals of Radio Interferometry course. The course is based on the ipython-based book of the same name. This site will be updated regularly.

A selection of presentations from previous years are available for download here, some of which has accompanying video files and can be streamed directly from YouTube

Course Schedule

  1. Radio Science - K. Thorat (odp)
  1. Limitations of Single Dish Astronomy (slides)
  1. Modern Interferometric Arrays (slides)
  1. Positional Astronomy - T. Grobler (slides)
  1. Fourier Theory and Discrete Fourier Transforms - G. Jozsa (slides)
  1. Visibility Space - J. Girard (slides)
  1. Instrumentation - G. Foster (slides)
  1. The Radio Interferometric Measurement Equation (RIME) - O. Smirnov (slides)
  1. Calibration - T. Grobler (slides)
